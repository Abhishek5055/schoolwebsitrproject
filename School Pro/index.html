About

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners and practise the core values of the school.

Government 10 Number School Vijayapura

The school consists of Grades from LKG to 10. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Kannada is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

To educate all students to the highest levels of academic achievement, to enable them to reach and expand their potential, and to prepare them to become productive, responsible, ethical, creative and compassionate members of society.
